## About Me
I am a first year PhD student at Leibniz University Hannover, Germany and a [MSCA](https://ec.europa.eu/research/mariecurieactions/node_en) fellow currently working on one of the projects in [CLEOPATRA](http://cleopatra-project.eu/) ITN. As a part of the ITN,  I am employed as a research assistant at [Visual Analytics Group](https://www.tib.eu/en/research-development/visual-analytics/), TIB and advised by [Dr. Ralph Ewerth](https://www.tib.eu/en/research-development/visual-analytics/staff/ralph-ewerth/). My current work and research revolves around interdesciplinary topics like deep/machine learning, multimodality with focus on Computer Vision and NLP, digital humanities and semiotics.
